Our Story

A Limerick Favourite

Nestled in the heart of Limerick on Russell Road, Russells Select Bar has been a beloved local institution for years. Whether you're calling in for a casual pint, a hearty lunch, or a special evening out — you'll always find a warm welcome here.

We pride ourselves on quality food made fresh daily, an exceptional drinks selection, and the kind of atmosphere that keeps people coming back. From our famous carvery to our à la carte menu, there's something for everyone at Russells.
